Blueberry Cake Recipe

Enjoy the burst of fresh, fruity flavor with our Blueberry Cake, a delightful treat perfect for any occasion. This cake combines the tender crumb of a classic vanilla cake with the vibrant taste of ripe blueberries. Each slice reveals a swirl of juicy blueberries that add a burst of sweetness and a beautiful color contrast. Whether served as a simple dessert or dressed up with a light glaze or a dollop of whipped cream, this Blueberry Cake is sure to be a hit with family and friends.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup milk
  • 1 ½ cups fresh or frozen blueberries
  • Optional: 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour (for coating blueberries)
  • Optional: Powdered sugar for dusting

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Set your oven to 350°F (175°C) and allow it to preheat while you prepare the cake batter.
  2. Prepare the Batter: In a large mixing bowl, cream together ½ cup softened butter and 1 cup granulated sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in 2 large eggs one at a time, then stir in 1 teaspoon vanilla extract. In a separate bowl, whisk together 1 ½ cups flour, 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder, and ¼ teaspoon salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture, alternating with ½ cup milk, until just combined.
  3. Fold in Blueberries: Toss the blueberries (and optional 2 tablespoons flour, if using) to coat them lightly. Gently fold the blueberries into the batter.
  4. Prepare the Pan: Grease and flour a 9-inch round cake pan or line it with parchment paper to prevent sticking.
  5. Pour and Bake: Pour the batter into the prepared pan and spread it evenly. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  6. Cool and Serve: Allow the cake to cool in the pan for 10 minutes before transferring it to a wire rack to cool completely. Dust with powdered sugar if desired before serving.
